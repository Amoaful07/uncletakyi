Fequently Asked Questions

Can I Order Other Colors of Sandals?

Yes. Sandals come in colors shown in the gallery. We craft for all colors and style.

In What Sizes Are The Sandals Available?

Our Sandals come in regular, medium-width sizes and various sizes for children.

What Size Do You Recommend for your Sandals?

We advice you to take the same size as those for elegant shoes, which is one size smaller than the sportive shoes. We recommend to compare the size of the soles in the Size Chart with the length of your feet.

I Have Wide Feet, Wil Your Sandals Fit ME? What size do you suggest?

The leather tends to stretch easily, and so if your feet are moderately big, tha sandals may initially seem to be tight, but in short time will adapt to your feet. If your feet are particularly wide, we suggest a size bigger than your usual size.

Do You Also Take Half-Size or Only Whole Size?

Half-measurement are not neccessary in sandals since they are open, adjustable with buckles, and leave a margin and allowance for the feet's movements.
